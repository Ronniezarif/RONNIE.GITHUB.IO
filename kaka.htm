Stop Bullying

Bullying is harmful behavior that targets individuals repeatedly over time. It can take many forms, including physical, verbal, and cyberbullying. Let's work together to stop bullying and create a safe environment for everyone.

If you or someone you know is experiencing bullying, don't hesitate to seek help from a trusted adult or authority figure. Remember, you're not alone, and there are people who care about you.
